Welcome to Sustainable Gardening

Discover eco-friendly ways to grow your own food and flowers. Our tips help you create a thriving garden while minimizing environmental impact.

Gardening Tips

Composting Basics

Turn kitchen scraps into nutrient-rich compost to enrich your soil naturally. Use a mix of green and brown materials for best results.

Water Conservation

Collect rainwater in barrels to irrigate your garden. Drip irrigation systems can also reduce water waste.

Native Plants

Choose plants native to your region to support local ecosystems and reduce maintenance needs.
